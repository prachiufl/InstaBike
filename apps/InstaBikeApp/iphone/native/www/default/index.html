Need Help? Try these FAQ
What if ride is stolen?
If a ride is stolen, the renter should attempt to get a police report.
What if ride is damaged?
Any damage occurring during a rental window is the sole responsibility of the renter.
What if someone is hurt?
In the rental agreement, all renters take responsibility for checking the bike for safety before taking it from the lister.
How do i exceed the rental?
If you are enjoying your rental and would like to keep the bike for a longer time period, you can request a new drop off date.
